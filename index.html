Real-time Simulation of Human Musculature

Hyderabad - India

Link to video showcasing results (https://youtu.be/-SYh3ZgB_vg)

We present a reduced model based on Position Based Dynamics(PBD) for real-time simulation of human musculature. We demonstrate our methods on the muscles of the human arm. Co-simulation of all the muscles of the human arm allow us to accurately track the development of stresses and strains in the muscles, when the arm is moved. We evaluate our method for accuracy by comparing it with gold standard simulation models based on finite volume methods, and demonstrate the stability of the method under flexion, extension and torsion.

Indian Conference on Computer Vision, Graphics and Image Processing (ICVGIP 2018), Hyderabad, India.
