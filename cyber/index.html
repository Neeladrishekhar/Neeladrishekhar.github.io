Academic training utilizing computers and internet

This is the landing page for the Cyber Academic Training facility, being offered in Lonavala for the upcoming academic years. The students will be receiving training primarily focusing around the IIT-JEE entrance and other similar examinations. The training will be organised by an IITian, while also covering the student's regular school work. Check the Announcements for all updates regularly. Have fun :)

This training provides an introduction to academic problem solving with computers using various internet sources like Wikipedia, Google books, educational YouTube channels, etc.
